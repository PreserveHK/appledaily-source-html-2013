拿破崙和獻媚者 - 古德明

In 1805, Napoleon defeated the Austrians and Russians at the Battle of Austerlitz. Upon his return to France, the inspector of medals presented him with silver medals illustrative of his victories. The first represented a French eagle holding an English lion. Napoleon was not pleased. "You flatterer!" he said. "You say the French eagle crushes the English lion, but I cannot put a fishing boat to sea that is not taken. I tell you it is the lion that strangles the eagle. Melt down the medal, and never bring me such another."
Napoleon found similar fault with the medal of Austerlitz. "Put 'Battle of Austerlitz' on one side, with the date; the French, Russian, and Austrian eagles on the other, without distinction. Posterity will distinguish the vanquished."
一八○五年，拿破崙於奧斯德立茲一役擊敗奧地利和俄羅斯聯軍，奏凱回到法國，法國勳章監督獻上銀章，顯示拿破崙輝煌的戰績。第一個銀章的圖形，是法國之鷹抓住英國之獅。拿破崙見了，並不高興，說道：「你只是拍馬屁！你說法國之鷹搏殺英國之獅，但我遣一艘漁船出海，都會落在英國手上。事實是英國之獅，正在扼殺法國之鷹。快把這銀章熔掉，不要再給我這樣的銀章。」
拿破崙對奧斯德立茲戰役的銀章也不滿意。他說：「銀章的一面，可刻『奧斯德立茲戰役』七字，以及日期；銀章另一面，則刻上法國、俄國、奧地利三鷹，不分高下。後世自然會分辨敗北的是哪一方。」
